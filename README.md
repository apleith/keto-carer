# keto-carer
A personal tool to help manage my keto without having to pay a subscription.
Dashboard
AI connected
Keto Menu planner
I want it to be hosted on my dev computer.
I want it to run off my Claude AI account (or a Local LLM with web access if that is better suited).

I want the multi-function "app" to manage all elements of my keto diet, including meal planning, grocery lists, and tracking my progress.

The app shoud be able to track multiple people (at least 2, up to 4?) so that I can manage my diet along with my family members.

I want it track medication and supplements as well, so that I can ensure I'm taking everything I need to support my keto lifestyle.I also want the app to have a feature for tracking my water intake, as staying hydrated is crucial on a keto diet.

I want the app to have a user-friendly interface that allows me to easily input my meals, snacks, and beverages throughout the day. It should also provide me with nutritional information for each item I log, so I can keep track of my macronutrient intake.

It should be aware of academic literature on the matters to ensure complianc with medications and proper food intakes. It should be able to take in lab results for further information.

Each profile should be able to rate ingredients on a scale of 1-10 based on how well they fit into their keto lifestyle, and the app should use this information to make personalized meal recommendations.

I'd then like the meal to be able to send regular notifcations to each members phone to remind them to log their meals, take their medications, and drink water... and any other notification types that would be helpful for managing a keto lifestyle.

Necessary pages/tabs include grocery lists, meal planning, progress tracking, medication/supplement tracking, and water intake tracking. Each page should be designed to be intuitive and easy to navigate.

I also want the backend of the app to regularly (1-2 times a week) to pull in new research and updates related to the keto diet, medications, and supplements, and update the app's recommendations and features accordingly. This will ensure that the app remains up-to-date with the latest scientific findings and continues to provide accurate and relevant information to its users.
